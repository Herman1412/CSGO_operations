Operation Breakout

About Operation Breakout

Operation Breakout was the fourth Counter-Strike: Global Offensive operation, following Operation Phoenix. It was introduced on the July 1, 2014 update at the original price of $5.99 USD/â‚¬4,99 EURO and concluded on October 2, 2014. Operation Breakout featured 6 new community maps, 3 exclusive weapon collections (Baggage, Cobble, and Overpass), an operation themed weapon case, the addition of operation missions and the Operation Journal. After the ESL One Cologne 2014 tournament, the Cache collection was made available as a potential mission reward collection. (Scroll the picture on the right to see the original blogpost)

Operation Mission

Introduced in Operation Breakout, the operation missions task the player with a goal to complete and reward the player with a random skin from one of the exclusive Operation Breakout weapon collections during the operation. Also introduced in Operation Breakout, the operation journal contains information about the player, the player's statistics during the operation and provides the player with a quick field guide of the featured maps.

Other information

  • The Counter-Terrorists featured in the title image are using a P90 | Asiimov and a UMP-45 | Labyrinth from the Operation Breakout Weapon Case and are standing in front of the house from the featured map, Insertion.
  • Operation Breakout was the first operation that did not get to be extended past the scheduled expiration date, and it ended on October 2, 2014.
  • Operation Breakout was the first operation to feature missions an a player journal
