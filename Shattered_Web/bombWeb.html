Studio

Studio (de_studio) is a community-created Bomb Defusal map designed by Hordeau, The Horse Strangler, ZeLz Storm, Hollandje and TanookiSuit3, and is featured in Operation Shattered Web in Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. Terrorists have infiltrated an American film studio through the stunt production company and are trying to destroy expensive filmsets and equipment
