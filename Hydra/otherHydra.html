Rialto

Rialto (gd_rialto) is an official Wingman map featured in Operation Hydra, available in Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. This map is placed in bridge under construction, sharing its theme with Canals. The Terrorists' target is a bomb site at the center of the bridge. The map is modeled and named after the Rialto Bridge in Venice, Italy.

Dizzy

Dizzy (ar_dizzy) is a Flying Scoutsman map added during Operation Hydra in Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. This map is placed in roof of skyscraper which is under construction, similar in theme to Vertigo. Some areas of the map are designed with gameplay for the Flying Scoutsman mode in mind, with high ledges that can be easily accessed due to the lowered gravity. There are also slopes on the sides useful for surfing, another act that is easy to perform in Flying Scoutsman.
