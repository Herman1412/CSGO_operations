Operation Bravo Coin

Bronce Coin

To get the bronce coin you had to buy the pass. The coin features a bronce sword and brown globe with bronce borders and autumn 2013, and CS:GO operation bravo written on it.

Silver Coin

To get the silver coin you had to play 10 hours (600 Minutes) and get 5 Competitive wins on Operation maps. The coin features a silver sword and brown globe with silver borders and autumn 2013, and CS:GO operation bravo written on it.

Gold Coin

To get the gold coin you had to play 30 hours (1800 Minutes) and get 15 Competitive wins on Operation maps. The coin features a gold sword and brown globe with gold borders and autumn 2013, and CS:GO operation bravo written on it.
