Bomb defusal maps in opperation Bravo

Ali

Ali (de_ali) is a custom bomb defusal map featured in Operation Bravo and Operation Phoenix from Counter-Strike: Global Offensive developed by Squad. On the October 13, 2016 Update, the map was removed from the game files, making Coast inaccessible via console.

Cache

Cache (de_cache) is a custom Bomb Defusal map featured in Operation Bravo and in Operation Phoenix, available in Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. Originally a custom map for Counter-Strike: Source, it was remade for Counter-Strike: Global Offensive with rebalances to fix the original's Terrorist-sided issues. The map takes place in Pripyat, Ukraine. The Chernobyl Power Plant is visible from the Terrorist spawn, along with nearby buildings and even fencing material consistent with the real-life counterpart.

Chinatown

Chinatown (de_chinatown) is a custom bomb defusal map featured in Operation Bravo designed by Skybex. The conflict is likely taking place in Chinatown of San Francisco or New York. On the October 13, 2016 Update, the map was removed from the game files, making Coast inaccessible via console.

Gwalior

Gwalior (de_gwalior) is a custom bomb defusal map featured in Operation Bravo. It was designed by FMPONE and kikette. The map takes place and is named after Gwalior, a real life city in India.

Ruins

Ruins (de_ruins) is a custom bomb defusal map featured in Operation Bravo. The map takes place in a Mayan temple.

Cobblestone

Cobblestone (de_cbble), formerly known as Cobble, is a bomb defusal map featured in the Counter-Strike series. It was added to Counter-Strike: Global Offensive in the Winter Offensive update alongside the map Overpass. Cobblestone is a large-sized map, and had quite significant vertical combat in its earlier iterations. In earlier versions, it is fairly easy to take fall damage due to the high height of many of the map's drops. Bots in early versions of the game will also occasionally fall to their deaths. Additionally, this map is open, making snipers very useful. The map was designed by David Johnston after completing Dust.

Overpass

Overpass (de_overpass) is a Bomb Defusal map for Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, released in the Winter Offensive update along with the map Cobblestone. The map's setting is a canal overpass and the park built above it in Berlin, Germany. The GSG-9 must protect a stalled military shipment on a canal overpass while the Phoenix Connexion can either attack the shipment head-on or attempt to destroy the overpass itself by bombing the pillar below.
