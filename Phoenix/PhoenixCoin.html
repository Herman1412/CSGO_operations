Bronce Coin

To get the bronce coin you had to buy the pass. The coin features a bronce phoenix and a orange background with bronce borders and winter 2014, and CS:GO operation phoenix written on it.

Silver Coin

To get the silver coin you had to play 10 Hours (600 Minutes) and get 5 Competitive wins on Operation maps. The coin features a silver phoenix and a orange background with silver borders and winter 2014, and CS:GO operation phoenix written on it.

Gold Coin

To get the gold coin you had to play 30 Hours (1800 Minutes) and get 15 Competitive wins on Operation maps. The coin features a gold phoenix and a orange background with gold borders and winter 2014, and CS:GO operation phoenix written on it.
