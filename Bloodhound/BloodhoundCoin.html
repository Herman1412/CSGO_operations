Operation Bloodhound Coin

Bronce Coin

To get the bronce coin you had to buy the pass. The coin features a bronce animal scull and 2 falchion knifes on a red and black background with bronce and red borders and spring 2015, and bloodhound written on it.

Silver Coin

To get the silver coin you had to accrue 9 stars. The coin features a silver animal scull and 2 falchion knifes on a red and black background with silver and red borders and spring 2015, and bloodhound written on it.

Gold Coin

To get the gold coin you had to accrue 14 stars. The coin features a gold and animal scull and 2 falchionknifes on a red and black background with gold and red borders and spring 2015, and bloodhound written on it.
